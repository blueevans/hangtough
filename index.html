HANG TOUGH FINE ART PRINTING, FRAMING & GALLERY

Welcome to Hang Tough, your local fine art printing & framing specialists. We offer a high end service to artists, galleries, and individual clients as well as working closely with interior designers, architects, curators and corporate clients. We have refined our range of fine art papers and frame styles to offer our customers a simple contemporary selection making the decision process quick, easy and professional. Hang Tough frames are custom made to order after an initial in-store consultation. Our front of house features the Hang Tough Gallery where we promote and exhibit some of the very best emerging Irish artists. We have framed and unframed prints and originals available to view and purchase - a perfect starting point for any curatorial project for your home or premises. Get in touch with us today to discuss your printing, framing or curatorial requirements.
